# Supply Chain Analytics

Analysis of supply chain data in R, covering revenue, stock levels, customer demographics, cost, and supplier performance.

## Overview

This project examines a supply chain dataset to understand where revenue and risk concentrate across product lines and suppliers.

## Objectives

- Understand revenue and stock levels by product type
- Profile customer demographics by product
- Assess shipping costs by carrier and defect rates by product type

## Tech stack

R

## Key findings

- Skincare products generate the highest revenue, indicating strong market demand.
- Shipping costs and defect rates vary meaningfully by carrier and supplier, pointing to concrete cost-reduction opportunities.

## Recommendations

- Explore alternatives to Carrier B to reduce shipping expenses
- Tighten quality control to reduce defects across product categories
- Renegotiate lead times with Supplier 4 for haircare products
